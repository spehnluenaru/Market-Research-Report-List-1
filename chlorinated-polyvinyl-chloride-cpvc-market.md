<p><p>Chlorinated Polyvinyl Chloride (CPVC) is a thermoplastic produced by chlorinating PVC resin, enhancing its thermal and chemical resistance. The CPVC market is divided into two main types: Injection Grade and Extrusion Grade. Injection Grade CPVC is primarily used for manufacturing complex shapes and components through injection molding processes. In contrast, Extrusion Grade CPVC is suited for producing continuous profiles and tubing via extrusion methods. Both grades cater to various industrial applications, including plumbing, fire protection, and industrial piping systems.</p></p>
