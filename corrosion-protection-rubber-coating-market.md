Soft rubber linings are typically flexible, providing excellent resistance to wear and chemical damage, making them ideal for protecting surfaces that experience dynamic movements. In contrast, hard rubber linings offer superior structural integrity and durability, suitable for static applications in harsh environments. Both types are essential for extending the lifespan of equipment and structures exposed to corrosive substances.</p></p>
