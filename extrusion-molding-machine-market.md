<p><p>The extrusion molding machine market is categorized into three main types: hydraulic, all-electric, and hybrid. Hydraulic machines utilize oil pressure for high forces, making them ideal for large-scale production and complex shapes. All-electric machines offer precision, energy efficiency, and lower maintenance, making them suitable for high-speed applications and smaller parts. Hybrid machines combine features of both hydraulic and electric systems, providing flexibility and energy savings, catering to diverse production needs while optimizing performance and efficiency.</p></p>
