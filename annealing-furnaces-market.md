<p><p>The annealing furnaces market is categorized into batch type and continuous type. Batch type furnaces process discrete quantities of materials in individual loads, allowing flexibility for various part sizes and compositions. They are ideal for smaller production volumes or specialized applications. In contrast, continuous type furnaces operate continuously, enabling high-volume processing with consistent temperature control. This type is efficient for large-scale operations, as materials move through the system without interruption, making it suitable for mass production and uniform product quality.</p></p>
