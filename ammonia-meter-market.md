<p><p>The ammonia meter market comprises three primary types: electrochemical, catalytic, and infrared ammonia meters. Electrochemical ammonia meters function by utilizing chemical reactions to detect ammonia levels, offering quick response times. Catalytic meters employ a catalyst to facilitate reactions that generate measurable signals, suitable for various industrial applications. Infrared ammonia meters utilize infrared light absorption for precise ammonia detection, ideal for continuous monitoring in challenging environments. Each type serves specific applications based on sensitivity, accuracy, and operational conditions.</p></p>
