<p><p>High-density racks are storage systems designed to maximize space utilization in warehouses. Drive-in racks allow forklifts to enter from one side, enabling the storage of multiple pallets deep, ideal for high-volume, low-variability inventory. Conversely, drive-through racks enable access from both sides, enhancing flexibility and allowing easier inventory rotation. Both systems prioritize efficiency and space optimization, catering to varying operational needs while accommodating high-density storage requirements in modern logistics and warehousing environments.</p></p>
