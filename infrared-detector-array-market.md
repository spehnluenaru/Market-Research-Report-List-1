<p><p>The infrared detector array market is primarily segmented into various types based on material composition. Lead Selenide (PbSe) detectors are suitable for thermal imaging applications due to their sensitivity to longer wavelengths. Lead Sulfide (PbS) detectors excel in low-cost applications where performance is acceptable in the mid-infrared range. Other materials include various semiconductor composites and specialized materials tailored for specific applications.</p></p>
