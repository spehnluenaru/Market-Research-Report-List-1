<p><p>The Sinusitis treatment medication market includes several key types. Nasal corticosteroids reduce inflammation, providing relief from symptoms. Antibiotics treat bacterial infections associated with sinusitis, though they are ineffective against viral forms. Other drugs may include antihistamines or mucolytics, which assist in managing symptoms and improving mucus clearance, enhancing overall treatment outcomes.</p></p>
