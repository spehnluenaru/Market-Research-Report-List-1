Rectangle type heaters are designed for applications requiring larger surface areas and even heat distribution, making them ideal for flat surfaces or uniform heating needs. In contrast, round type heaters are typically used in applications with curved surfaces or specific heat spots, providing versatility for various industrial and commercial uses. Both types benefit from silicone’s durability, flexibility, and excellent thermal properties, catering to diverse heating requirements.</p></p>
