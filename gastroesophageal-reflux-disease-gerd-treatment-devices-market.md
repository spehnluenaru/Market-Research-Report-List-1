<p><p>The Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease (GERD) treatment devices market primarily includes fundoplication devices and implant devices. Fundoplication devices assist in surgical procedures that wrap the upper stomach around the lower esophagus to prevent reflux. Implant devices, such as magnetic sphincter augmentation devices, are designed to enhance lower esophageal sphincter function. Both types aim to alleviate reflux symptoms, improve patients' quality of life, and reduce dependence on lifelong medication, catering to various severity levels and patient needs in GERD management.</p></p>
