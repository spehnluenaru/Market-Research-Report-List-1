<p><p>Digital commerce platforms facilitate various market types. Business to Consumer (B2C) involves businesses selling directly to consumers, often seen in online retail. Business to Business (B2B) focuses on transactions between businesses, such as wholesale suppliers selling to retailers. Consumer to Business (C2B) allows individuals to offer products or services to businesses, like freelance services. Consumer to Consumer (C2C) enables individuals to sell to one another, often through platforms that support peer-to-peer transactions, like auction sites and online marketplaces.</p></p>
