<p><p>The mineral flame retardants market is divided into three main types: ordinary minerals, clay minerals, and others. Ordinary minerals, such as talc and calcium carbonate, provide basic fire resistance due to their natural properties. Clay minerals, including bentonite and montmorillonite, enhance thermal stability and barrier protection. "Others" encompass various specialized minerals like vermiculite and mica, which offer unique flame-retardant characteristics. These mineral types are crucial for improving fire safety in construction, textiles, and automotive applications.</p></p>
