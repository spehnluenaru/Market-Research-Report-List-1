<p><p>The electroplating additives market comprises several key types, including brightener agents, surfactants, and wetting agents. Brightener agents enhance the smoothness and brightness of the plated surface, improving aesthetic and functional properties. Surfactants reduce surface tension, promoting better adhesion and dispersion of the metal deposition. Wetting agents facilitate the spreading of the plating solution, ensuring even coating across surfaces. Together, these additives optimize the electroplating process, resulting in higher quality finishes and improved performance characteristics of plated materials.</p></p>
