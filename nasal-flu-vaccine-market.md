<p><p>The nasal flu vaccine market comprises primarily two types: trivalent and quadrivalent vaccines. The trivalent flu vaccine targets three strains of the influenza virus, typically two influenza A strains and one B strain, providing a broader immunity in more common flu seasons. On the other hand, the quadrivalent flu vaccine covers four strains, adding an additional B strain, which enhances protection and is particularly beneficial during seasons with varied circulating strains. Both types are effective but serve different vaccination needs.</p></p>
