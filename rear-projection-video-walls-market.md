<p><ul><li>LED-lit</li><li>Laser-lit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rear projection video walls are divided into two main market types: LED-lit and laser-lit. LED-lit video walls utilize light-emitting diodes to produce vibrant, high-contrast images, making them suitable for environments with varying lighting conditions. In contrast, laser-lit video walls leverage laser technology for enhanced brightness and color accuracy, resulting in superior image quality and longer lifespan. Both types cater to different needs, with LED being ideal for dynamic displays and laser offering exceptional performance for critical viewing applications.</p></p>
