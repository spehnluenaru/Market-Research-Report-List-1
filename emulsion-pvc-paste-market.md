<p><p>The Emulsion PVC Paste Market is primarily segmented into two types: the Micro Suspension Method and the Emulsion Method. The Micro Suspension Method involves dispersing PVC in small particles within a solvent, enhancing stability and uniformity, making it suitable for applications like coatings and adhesives. Conversely, the Emulsion Method utilizes water as a dispersing medium, resulting in a versatile product with a broader range of applications, including films and sealants. Both methods cater to diverse functional needs in various industries.</p></p>
