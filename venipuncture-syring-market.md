<p><p>The venipuncture syringe market comprises various types designed for specific medical applications. Disposable sterile injection needles are utilized for single-use injections, ensuring hygiene and reducing infection risk. Disposable intravenous infusion needles facilitate the administration of fluids and medications directly into the bloodstream. Disposable fiber needles provide enhanced visibility and flexibility for precise access to veins, while venous indwelling needles are used for prolonged access, allowing for continuous medication delivery without repeated punctures, thus improving patient comfort.</p></p>
