<p><strong>The Cataract Surgical Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intraocular Lenses (IOLs)</li><li>Ophthalmic Viscoelastic Devices (OVDs)</li><li>Phacoemulsification Devices</li><li>Cataract Surgery Lasers</li><li>IOL Injectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cataract surgical devices market includes several key types. Intraocular lenses (IOLs) are artificial lenses implanted to restore vision post-surgery. Ophthalmic viscoelastic devices (OVDs) maintain space in the eye during procedures and protect tissues. Phacoemulsification devices break up and aspirate cloudy lens material using ultrasound. Cataract surgery lasers enhance precision and outcomes in the surgery process. IOL injectors facilitate the effortless insertion of IOLs into the eye, streamlining the surgical workflow and improving patient experience.</p></p>
