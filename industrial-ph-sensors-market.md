<p><p>The industrial pH sensors market comprises several types, including glass type sensors, ISFET sensors, and others. Glass type sensors are traditional and utilize a glass membrane to measure hydrogen ion activity, offering high accuracy. ISFET sensors, or Ion-Sensitive Field-Effect Transistors, provide a more robust alternative suitable for harsh environments and real-time monitoring. The "others" category includes advanced technologies like solid-state sensors and optical sensors, catering to specific applications where conventional methods may not suffice, enhancing measurement versatility and efficiency.</p></p>
