<p><strong>The Heart Catheterization Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Angiography Catheters</li><li>Guiding Catheters</li><li>Pulmonary Artery Catheters</li><li>PTCA Balloon Catheters</li><li>Intravascular Ultrasound (IVUS) Catheters</li><li>Electrophysiology Catheters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heart catheterization market comprises various catheter types, each serving distinct diagnostic and therapeutic purposes. Angiography catheters visualize blood vessels, while guiding catheters facilitate the delivery of tools to specific heart areas. Pulmonary artery catheters monitor heart function and pressure. PTCA balloon catheters are used in angioplasty procedures to open blocked arteries. Intravascular ultrasound (IVUS) catheters provide real-time imaging of vessel walls, and electrophysiology catheters assess electrical activity in the heart to diagnose arrhythmias.</p></p>
