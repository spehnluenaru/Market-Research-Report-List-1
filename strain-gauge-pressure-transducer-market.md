<p><strong>The Strain Gauge Pressure Transducer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resistive</li><li>Capacitive</li><li>Optics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Strain gauge pressure transducers utilize different technologies to measure pressure based on changes in physical properties. </p><p>Resistive transducers change resistance due to deformation of a strain gauge, offering high accuracy. Capacitive transducers use changes in capacitance to detect pressure variations, providing a wider range of applications. Optical pressure transducers rely on light modulation through strain effects, offering high sensitivity and immunity to electromagnetic interference. Each type serves distinct industries, including aerospace, automotive, and industrial automation, catering to specific measurement needs.</p></p>
