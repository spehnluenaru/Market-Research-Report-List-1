<p><p>The Kraft Paper Tape market is primarily divided into two types: water-free and wet Kraft Paper Tape. Water-free Kraft Paper Tape is made using a pressure-sensitive adhesive that does not require moisture for activation, making it convenient and easy to use for various packaging applications. In contrast, wet Kraft Paper Tape requires water to activate the adhesive, providing a stronger bond once applied. Both types are eco-friendly, recyclable, and popular in sustainable packaging solutions, catering to diverse consumer needs in various industries.</p></p>
