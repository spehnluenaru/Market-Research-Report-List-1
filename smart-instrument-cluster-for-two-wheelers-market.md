<p><p>Smart instrument clusters for two-wheelers encompass three main types: LCD clusters, TFT clusters, and hybrid display clusters. LCD clusters use liquid crystal technology for basic information display, providing clear visibility but limited graphics. TFT clusters leverage thin-film transistor technology for vibrant colors and enhanced readability, allowing for more advanced functionalities and user interfaces. Hybrid display clusters combine both LCD and TFT elements, offering a balance between simplicity and advanced graphics, catering to diverse rider preferences and enhancing the overall riding experience.</p></p>
