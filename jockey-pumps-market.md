<p><p>The jockey pumps market encompasses two primary types: centrifugal pumps and turbine pumps. Centrifugal pumps utilize rotational energy to move fluid, offering high flow rates and efficiency, making them suitable for fire protection and water supply systems. In contrast, turbine pumps are designed for lifting water from deep sources, using a series of impellers, ideal for applications requiring high pressure and volume, such as irrigation and municipal water distribution. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining water pressure and ensuring system reliability.</p></p>
