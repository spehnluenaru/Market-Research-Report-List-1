<p><p>LIMS (Laboratory Information Management System) software is categorized into cloud-based and web-based markets. Cloud-based LIMS is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access data anytime, anywhere, providing scalability and automatic updates. In contrast, web-based LIMS runs on a web browser but may not necessarily utilize cloud infrastructure, often requiring local installation for certain features. Both types enhance laboratory efficiency but differ in deployment and access flexibility, catering to various user needs and organizational sizes.</p></p>
