<p><p>The Spiral CT scan machine market includes two main types: Double Layer Helical CT Scanners and Multi-Layer Helical CT Scanners. Double Layer Helical CT Scanners utilize two rows of detectors to capture images quickly, enhancing speed and image quality for routine diagnostics. In contrast, Multi-Layer Helical CT Scanners feature multiple layers of detectors, allowing for even faster imaging and higher resolution, making them suitable for complex cases and advanced applications such as 3D reconstructions and detailed anatomical studies.</p></p>
