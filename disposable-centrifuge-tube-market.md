<p><p>The disposable centrifuge tube market is primarily categorized into two types: round bottom centrifuge tubes and conical centrifuge tubes. Round bottom tubes are ideal for general laboratory applications, providing uniform sedimentation and minimizing sample loss. In contrast, conical centrifuge tubes feature a tapered design that facilitates the easy collection of precipitates and cell pellets, making them suitable for applications requiring sample retrieval. Both types are widely used in research, clinical, and industrial settings for their convenience and sterility.</p></p>
