<p><p>Cyanate ester resin glass fiber prepreg is available in two primary market types: thermoset and thermoplastic. Thermoset prepregs are characterized by their permanent chemical bonds formed during curing, offering excellent thermal and chemical resistance, ideal for high-performance applications. In contrast, thermoplastic prepregs can be reshaped upon heating, providing enhanced flexibility and recyclability. Both types serve diverse industries, including aerospace, automotive, and electronics, where strength, durability, and lightweight properties are critical. Their unique properties cater to varying manufacturing and application needs.</p></p>
