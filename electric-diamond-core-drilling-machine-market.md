<p><strong>The Electric Diamond Core Drilling Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Type</li><li>Dry Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric diamond core drilling machines are categorized into two main types: wet and dry. Wet-type machines utilize water to cool the drill bit and remove debris, allowing for smoother cutting and reduced dust generation, making them ideal for harder materials. Conversely, dry-type machines operate without water, relying on vacuum systems to manage dust, making them suitable for indoor or confined spaces. Both types are essential in construction, mining, and mineral exploration, catering to different operational needs and environmental conditions.</p></p>
