<p><ul><li>Remote Eye Trackers</li><li>Head-Mounted Eye Trackers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The eye tracking devices market is primarily divided into two types: remote eye trackers and head-mounted eye trackers. Remote eye trackers use a camera setup to capture eye movement from a distance, allowing users to maintain a natural posture while interacting with screens. In contrast, head-mounted eye trackers are worn on the user's head, providing a more immersive experience by tracking eye movements in relation to the user's view of the environment. Both types are essential for various applications, including research, gaming, and accessibility.</p></p>
