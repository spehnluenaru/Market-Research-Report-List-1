<p><p>The Photonic Crystal Fiber Laser market comprises Continuous Wave (CW) and Pulsed Fiber Lasers. CW Fiber Lasers provide a constant output of light, ideal for applications requiring steady power, such as materials processing and telecommunications. In contrast, Pulsed Fiber Lasers emit light in short bursts, offering high peak power, making them suitable for precision tasks like marking, engraving, and medical applications. Both types leverage the unique properties of photonic crystal fibers, enhancing efficiency, versatility, and performance in various industries.</p></p>
