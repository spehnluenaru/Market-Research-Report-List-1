<p><p>The LED protectors market comprises several types, primarily including shunt protectors and surge protectors. Shunt protectors are designed to safeguard LEDs from overcurrent and prevent thermal runaway, ensuring longevity and stable performance. Surge protectors, on the other hand, shield LEDs from voltage spikes caused by lightning or power surges, preserving their functionality and reducing damage risk. Other types may encompass fuse-based protectors or specialized circuits, each serving to enhance the reliability and efficiency of LED lighting systems across various applications.</p></p>
